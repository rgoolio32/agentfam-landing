MyFam SMS Messaging Program

Program name: AgentFam · Operated by MyFam (Gole Enterprise LLC) · fam.rahulgole.com

What this program is

AgentFam sends transactional SMS messages to family members so a family’s AI assistant (“Fammie”) can deliver reminders, calendar alerts, task notifications, and a daily morning summary. Messages are sent only to phone numbers that have opted in using one of the methods below.

How to opt in (Call to Action)

There are two ways to opt in to AgentFam SMS messages:

  1. Admin-initiated invite with member confirmation. A family administrator signs up at fam.rahulgole.com, adds a family member’s name and phone number, and checks a required attestation box confirming the member has agreed to receive AgentFam SMS notifications. The new member then receives a one-time SMS with a magic link. Tapping the link opens a preferences page where the member reviews the message types (reminders, alerts, daily summaries) and confirms or declines. The number is marked opted-in only after the member confirms on that preferences page.
  2. Direct keyword opt-in. A user texts START, YES, or JOIN to the AgentFam number. They receive an automated confirmation reply describing the program, message frequency, fees, and the opt-out keyword.

In both flows, consent is logged with timestamp, IP address (where available), and the consent method.
